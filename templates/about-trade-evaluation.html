About Trade Evaluation

Hi, this is Trade Evaluation, a personal project that aims at demonstrating my ability to combine IT skills with financial knowledge to prospective recruiters. This platform provides investors a set of tools for analyses and visualization of investment performance.

Managing Hypothetical Portfolio

Concept: For a new account open, user is provided with hypothetical portolios of cash for each of the markets that the application supports, namely Germany, the U.S., and Vietnam.
Users can implement their own trading strategies, making decisions on buying or selling which stocks. The application will help them evaluate and visualize the performance of their portfolio.
Below is a brief guideline to each function of the site.

Get Quote

This function allows users to query information about the stock and to check whether the symbol they type in is valid.
To use the function, click Quote on the top menu bar, then type in the symbol of the stock and select its market.

Make Transactions

To make a transaction, users can click Trade on the top menu bar. Here they first need to select whether they want to buy or sell stocks, then fill in the relevant information and click Buy/Sell.
At the moment, the app will execute the trade at current market price and allow no short-sell or leverage. More options may be intergrated in the future releases.

See Transaction History

To view transaction history, users can click History on the top menu bar.
The entire trading history will be presented in the table. Users can filter each market by selecting the respective portfolio.
In the future releases, more filter options may be integrated.

View Current Portfolios

This function enables users to update the status quo of their portfolios.
To visit this tab, users first need to click Portfolio on the top menu bar. Here data on every individual holding and on portfolio level is presented. The cost price is calculated using weighted average method and returns are derived accordingly.
At the moment, no cash deposit or withdrawal is allowed. The future releases may incorporate these options to make the hypothetical portfolio more realistic.

Performance Evaluation

This is the core function of the platform, which aims at enabling investors a tool to conduct "comprehensive" analyses on their portfolio performance.
For the current version, users can click Evaluation tab on top, selecting the market that they wish to review historical performance, and selecting the respective benchmark against which they want to compare their performance.
In the future releases, multiple further analyses may be provided. See Future Updates for more details.

Future Updates

Beside the additional options mentioned above, adding new features in the core function Performance Evaluation will be the main focus for the future updates:

  • Factor-based analyses (using 5-factor model and estimating portfolio's alpha);
  • Measurement of turnover, liquidity, risks, and risk-adjusted returns;
  • Option to upload real portfolio data in excel format to obtain performance evaluation from the platform.
